I am an undergraduate student at the [University of Massachusetts Amherst](https://www.umass.edu/) pursuing a Bachelor's of Science degree in [Computer Science](https://www.cics.umass.edu/). Upon completion, I have been invited by the university to continue my studies through participation in an accelerated master's program. I also plan on pursuing a PhD past my Master's degree.

My research interests lie at the intersection of machine learning and healthcare. In particular, I care about understanding the underlying data generating process of various medical conditions (causal discovery) as well as generatively modeling medical images to find early onsets of diagnoses.

Currently, I work as a Machine Learning intern as part of the Research & Development team at [IOMICS Corporation](https://www.iomics.us/). The focus of my work is to better understand causal discovery/inference algorithms and how those algorithms can be easily accessed and automated through the use of existing tools ([DoWhy](https://www.pywhy.org/dowhy/v0.11.1/) & [causal-learn](https://causal-learn.readthedocs.io/en/latest/)).
